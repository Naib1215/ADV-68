Mohammad Mohaimin Uddin Naib was born on 15th April, 2005 at Royal Hospital - situated in Chittagong, Bangladesh. From his childhood, he was introduced to the world of fantasy and adventure, especially knowledge. He moved in to Dhaka, with his family and got enrolled into DPS STS SCHOOL DHAKA, on around 2012. He consistently ranked himself amongst the top three in his class, and his earliest academic brakthrough came when he was double-promoted to a higher class, that has accompanied him till now in his academic venture. Growing old, he took part in many extra-curricular activities, namely: Art, Chess, Table-tennis, MUN, Programming etc. Some of his achievements include: Third position in the 7th Edition of Bangla Olympiad, Outstanding Judge award in DPS MUN V, Runners-Up in DPS Interschool Chess Championship, Runners-Up in Sub-Junior Divsion of Inter-school Pallima Sangshad Table-tennis tournament. Likewise, his biggest Chess breakthrough came on, when he received his International FIDE rating. Currently he is enrolled with Byju's Programming course, and is opting to found his career in Software Engineering or International Relations.
